Bazaar: The Trading App

Become the very best there ever was by trading with players in your area.

App Store Play Store

Here's how it works.

  • 1. Browse
  • 2. Chat
  • 3. Trade

Browse for mons by using your feed. Only mons in your area show up. When you like one, tap on it!

Check the deets on this mon. If they're up to your standards, press the Chat button to talk to the trainer who posted it.

Once you and the other trainer settle on an dealio, coordinate the trade!

Browse the feed

The feed contains listings added by other players in your area. At any given second, you can pull down to refresh and see the most recent listings. Our advice: if you see a rare one, be quick!

  • Charizard
  • Lapras
  • Dragonite

Create listings of your own

It takes a few seconds to post a mon you have for trade, and you can add up to 25 of them. If they're not all Pidgeys, other trainers will reach out to you with what they have to offer.

Chat

Once you find that perfect mon to take down your entire city's gyms, chat with the trainer who owns it!
